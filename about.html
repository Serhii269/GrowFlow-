About

Why we built GrowFlow

Most productivity apps feel like pressure. Crossed-out lists, red streaks, failed goals. We wanted something different — something that felt alive.

GrowFlow started as a school project with a simple idea: what if your habits grew like plants? Not punishing you for missing a day, but rewarding you gently for showing up.

The result is a space where progress feels natural, organic, and worth coming back to.
